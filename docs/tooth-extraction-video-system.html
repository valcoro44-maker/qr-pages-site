Tooth Extraction Video System

1. Introduction (10 sec)

Today we'll explain what to expect during and after a tooth extraction.

2. Why it's needed (15 sec)

A tooth may need to be removed due to infection, damage, or crowding.

3. What to expect during the procedure (20-30 sec)

You will be numb.

You'll feel pressure, not pain.

The procedure is usually quick.

4. Post-op

Do: Bite on gauze for 30-45 minutes. Use ice packs. Take medications as prescribed.

Avoid: No straws. No smoking. No spitting forcefully.

5. Warning Signs

Call us if you experience severe pain after 2-3 days, excessive bleeding, or swelling that worsens.

6. Reassurance

Some discomfort is normal. We are here for you. Please call us if you have any concerns.
